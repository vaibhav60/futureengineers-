Curriculum

Choose Your Mission

Three progressive tracks designed to take your child from curious beginner to championship-ready engineer.

AGES 6–8

Beginner Robotics

A playful introduction to building and programming using mBot. No experience needed — just curiosity!

  • 8-week course · 1 session/week
  • mBot robot building
  • Drag-and-drop coding (Scratch)
  • Sensors & simple circuits
  • Certificate on completion
$120 / session
AGES 8–12

Intermediate Robotics

Dive into Arduino, sensors, and real programming. Build robots that solve real-world problems.

  • 10-week course · 1 session/week
  • Arduino microcontrollers
  • C++ programming basics
  • Ultrasonic & IR sensors
  • Mini competition at end
$120 / session
AGES 12–16

Advanced + Olympiad Prep

Competitive robotics training. Advanced electronics, algorithms, and full Olympiad preparation.

  • 12-week intensive · 2 sessions/week
  • ROS & advanced C++
  • PID control & computer vision
  • Official Olympiad prep
  • Team competition entry
$120 / session

Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before enrolling.

No experience needed for Beginner or Intermediate tracks. We start from absolute zero and build up. For Advanced track, some basic programming familiarity is helpful but not required.
Nothing! All robots, components, laptops, and materials are provided by the lab. Students just need to show up ready to learn. We do offer optional take-home kits for purchase.
We record all sessions and make them available to enrolled students. Missed classes can be made up in another time slot within the same week, subject to availability. We allow up to 2 make-up classes per course.
Payments are made per-session via PayPal (credit/debit card also accepted). You pay at the time of booking. We also offer multi-session bundles with a 10% discount. Refunds are available up to 48 hours before the class.
We keep class sizes small — maximum 8 students per session — to ensure every child gets individual attention from the instructor. This is intentional and non-negotiable for us.
Our main lab is located in the Chicago area. We also offer online sessions for students unable to attend in person. Online students receive a starter kit shipped to their home before their first class.
